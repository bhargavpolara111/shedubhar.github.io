Shedubhar

Shedubhar (Ta. Amreli) is a village in Amreli taluka in Amreli district in the Saurashtra region of Gujarat state in the western part of India. The main occupation of the people of Shedubhar village is agriculture , farm labor and animal husbandry . In this village mainly wheat , cumin , groundnut , sesame , millet , chickpea , cotton , diwali , rajko and other vegetable crops are cultivated . In this villageFacilities like primary school , panchayat house , anganwadi , primary health center as well as milk dairy are available. The village is situated on the banks of the river Thebi.
